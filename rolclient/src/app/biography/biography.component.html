ABOUT

River of Life Church House of Restoration is a Bible based, Christo-Centric Ministry plant that teaches Biblical principles to parishioners and models a 21st Century Ministry in a Post Modern Society. Our flavor of Worship is Pentecostal however, we create an atmosphere through our Worship Experience that makes healing and deliverance available to all who bask in the very presence of God. Join us for one of our Worship Experiences. We are located at 2149 North Avenue, Sacramento, CA 95838. Sunday Morning at 9:00 am.

The pedagogy of our ministry is free of heresy

Please give us a try, you will be glad you did. If you cannot make it to our assembly, feel free to watch our Facebook Live Podcast every Sunday Morning @ 9:00 AM.
