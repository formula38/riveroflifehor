DEEPEN YOUR SPIRITUAL JOURNEY

Congratulations on embarking on your Christian journey! But where do you go from here? We're here to guide you as you grow in your faith and embrace the purpose God has for you. If you're seeking the next steps in your journey, here's how we can assist you:

Next Steps
Classes

These empowering sessions, formerly known as the Purple Book, are tailored to help believers construct their lives upon the foundation of the Word and cultivate spiritual maturity. Delve into topics such as prayer and worship, faith and hope, spiritual hunger, and God's Word. Whether you're new to the faith or a seasoned believer, these classes offer a profound opportunity for growth. Completion of these classes is required for membership at River of Life, although membership is not a prerequisite for attendance.

Classes are held during our Mid-week Renewal format on Wednesdays at 6pm.

Membership Classes
If you've been a part of River of Life for some time and are considering membership, our membership classes are designed just for you. Discover the history, values, and vision of River of Life Church in these informative sessions.

Held quarterly on Saturday mornings, stay tuned for updates on the next class schedule.

Baptism

Baptism is a significant step in declaring our faith in Jesus Christ. It symbolizes our spiritual rebirth and commitment to following Him. This outward display of our inward faith is vital for every believer. Join us for baptisms held quarterly throughout the year.
