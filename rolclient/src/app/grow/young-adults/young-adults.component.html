YOUNG ADULTS

Welcome to ROLYA, our vibrant Young Adults Ministry!

For those aged 18 to 35, ROLYA is a dynamic community tailored just for you.

Situated in an area brimming with youthful energy, our ministry is crafted to embrace individuals from various backgrounds and life stages. Whether you've recently graduated or are starting a family, there's a place for you here!
Join us each month for engaging events designed to foster connections, deepen your faith, and spark joy. We're eager to meet you and journey together towards spiritual growth and fulfillment.

Meet our Leaders, Maurice + Erianna Scott

Guiding ROLYA is Maurice & Erianna Scott, along with a devoted team of leaders,
all fueled by a burning desire to witness the transformative power of the Gospel in the lives and futures of young adults.
