YOUNG ADULTS

ROLYA is our Young Adults Ministry

For 18 Years &Graduated - 35 Years Old
We live in a unique community where we have one of the youngest populations in the country! ROLYA is specifically designed for young adults from all walks of life.  From newly graduated to those with young families, we have something for you! Each month we host events designed to help you connect with others, grow in your faith, and have fun! We can't wait to meet you soon.

Meet our Leaders, Maurice + Erianna Scott

ROLYA is led by Maurice &Erianna Scott and a team of excellent leaders, 
with a passion to see the lives and destinies of young adults changed by the power of the Gospel.
