RIVER MEN

"Be vigilant, stand strong in your faith, act with courage, and be empowered by the Spirit."

1 Corinthians 16:13
Welcome to the River Men's Fellowship, a sanctuary for every man seeking to deepen his walk with the Lord. Under the guidance of Pastor Chris Phillips, our mission is to cultivate a community of men who are steadfast leaders in the Kingdom of God.

In the transient landscape of military communities where River of Life resides, our ministry plays a crucial role. Men often face sudden deployments and relocations, disrupting their families and routines. Our aim is to provide unwavering support and spiritual nourishment through every twist and turn of life's journey.

We offer:

An annual empowering conference
Men's fellowship gatherings
Engaging Bible studies
Connections tailored to our military brethren
