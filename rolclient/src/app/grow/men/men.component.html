RIVER MEN

Be watchful, stand firm in the faith, act like men, be strong.

1 Corinthians 16:13
The River Men ’s Ministry is here for every guy.  Led by Pastor Chris Phillips, the goal of the ministry is to raise up a group of men that are leaders for the Kingdom of God.

Because of the transient nature of the military towns in which River of Life is based, this is especially important.  Guys are many times deployed and uprooted from their families and homes at any given moment.  Our mission is to support and bless guys in every stage of life.

We offer:

A yearly conference
Men ’s night out events
Bible studies
Military Connections
