In the spirit of Pentecost, we affirm that children possess the divine capacity to intimately encounter God, to serve Him, and to journey alongside Him from tender years. We fervently believe that even in their innocence, children wield the power to radiate God's light in their spheres, influencing families, friendships, and schools for His glory.

As stewards of God's precious ones, we are committed to nurturing their inherent potential. River Kids Zone stands resolute in equipping our children to embrace their God-given destinies.

In accordance with the scriptural wisdom of Proverbs 22:6, we heed the call to train our children in the path of righteousness, knowing that as they mature, they will remain steadfast in their faith.
In our commitment to safeguarding and nurturing your precious children, we uphold rigorous standards for all our volunteers, ensuring:

Completion of a thorough River Kids Zone application
Comprehensive background record checks
Verification of four references
Completion of Guardian System training
Active membership in the River of Life community

To ensure the utmost safety and security of your child, only parents are authorized to check in or check out their child. For added protection, children are not released to older siblings. Additionally, each visiting parent receives a unique identification number, which will be promptly displayed in the sanctuary if their child needs attention.

Frequently Asked Questions
When does the River Kids Zone come alive?
The River Kids Zone springs to life at 6:50pm every Wednesday during Grow Time and at 9:45am on Sundays.
Will the River Kids Zone team notify me if my little one needs me?
Rest assured, we'll promptly contact parents via phone or text should their child require their attention during service.
What's the procedure for picking up my child?
Ensuring your child's safety is paramount. Only parents are permitted to check in or check out their child. We do not release children to older siblings.
Is there a space for nursing mothers to nurture their babies?
Absolutely! We've designated a Nursing Mom's Room located on the left side of the sanctuary. Complete with a speaker system, moms can remain connected to the service while tending to their little ones.
