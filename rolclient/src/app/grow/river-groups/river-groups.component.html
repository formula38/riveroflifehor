RIVER GROUPS


River Fellowships are gatherings dedicated to holistic growth for every member of the family. We eagerly welcome individuals of all ages, genders, marital statuses, and backgrounds to unite under the banner of Jesus. This inclusivity is paramount, as it fosters mutual enrichment between the seasoned and the youthful, the married and the single. Married couples exemplify godly unions for singles, while parents uplift and support one another in their journeys.

River Fellowship meetings are hosted in homes scattered throughout our community.

We offer three distinct types of River Fellowships:

Household Fellowships
Sisters' Circles
Brothers' Bible Studies
Men's Bible Study

Fellowships convene from February to November 2024

Household Fellowships
Household Fellowships serve as vibrant hubs for nurturing spiritual bonds and deepening faith. They cater to all, regardless of age, marital status, family structure, or spiritual maturity. These gatherings integrate segments from River of Life sermons, enabling participants to engage with messages on a profound and personal level. Household Fellowships play a pivotal role in preserving the intimacy of our community as we expand, empowering us to advance the Kingdom of Heaven.

Join a Household Fellowship today to embark on a journey of spiritual growth and fellowship.

Sisters' Circles
Sisters' Circles are tailor-made for women like you. Hosted by devoted women from River of Life Church, these groups convene across Jacksonville and its environs. From enriching Bible studies to impactful outreach endeavors, our diverse range of Sisters' Circles caters to various age groups and interests. There's undoubtedly a circle that resonates with your heart and soul.

Brothers' Bible Studies
Brothers' Bible Studies offer a sacred space for men to delve into the depths of God's Word and grow in spiritual maturity. Led by dedicated brothers from River of Life Church, these studies are held at various locations in Jacksonville and neighboring areas. Whether you're seeking to deepen your understanding of Scripture, build meaningful relationships with other men of faith, or explore practical applications of biblical principles in everyday life, our Brothers' Bible Studies provide a supportive and enriching environment. Join a Brothers' Bible Study today and embark on a transformative journey of spiritual growth and fellowship.
