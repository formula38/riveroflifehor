Proverbs 31:25
We believe that the Lord is raising up a company of women that are captivated by His voice. In a time where so many things are demanding our attention, we are determined to hear and obey His voice! We have a passion to join with and love our sisters and run together with the Good News of Jesus. Led by Pastor Miriam Phillips, ROL Women is a voice in the Eastern region of North Carolina and around the world.

Pastor Miriam, Senior Associate Pastor

Pastor Miriam Phillips is a dynamic speaker and teacher who delivers the Word of God in a practical way. Having grown up on the mission field, Pastor Miriam has a passion for the nations and has traveled all over the world teaching and preaching the Gospel. As head of ROL Women and the Prophetic Ministry at River of Life, Pastor Miriam also has a desire to see women rise up and fulfill the purpose God has for them in every area of their lives.
