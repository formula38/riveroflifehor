Pastor Miriam Phillips, Lead Shepherdess

A beacon of divine revelation, Pastor Miriam Phillips kindles the flames of revival with her anointed teachings. Nurtured in the crucible of missionary endeavors, Pastor Miriam's fervor for the nations has propelled her to proclaim the Gospel across the globe. As steward of Rivers of Grace Women's Fellowship and the Prophetic Ministry at River of Life, Pastor Miriam champions the ascent of women into their God-ordained destinies, empowering them to radiate His glory in every sphere of life.
