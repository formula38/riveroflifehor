R Youth stands as the vibrant Junior and Senior High ministry of River of Life Church. Guided by Maurice & Erianna Scott and a dynamic team of leaders, R Youth exists to witness the transformative power of the Gospel shaping the lives and futures of students. From our fervent praise and worship gatherings to our fervent prayers and acts of service, our students are engaged in mutual edification.
