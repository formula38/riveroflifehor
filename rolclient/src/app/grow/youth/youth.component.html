R Youth is the Junior and Senior High ministry of River of Life Church. Led by Maurice &Erianna Scott and a team of excellent leaders, the purpose of R Youth  is to see the lives and destinies of students changed by the power of the Gospel.  From our praise and worship team to our prayer and usher teams our students are serving one another.
