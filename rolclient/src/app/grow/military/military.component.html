MILITARY

Our mission is to serve the service members and their families by keeping them connected to God and the church at ROL while they are serving our country during deployment.

Our Military Deployed Support Group is united in connecting our Military service members and their families to the body here at ROL through the sharing of care packages for the deployed family member and phone calls, texts and quarterly meetings with spouses. The ministry is geared to ROL service members and their families. If you are a service member or spouse of a service member, let us help you stay connected with the body here at ROL.

Meet Our Leader
Shirley Russell heads up our Military Deployed Support Group. She is a Marine Corps veteran's wife and has a passion for our service members and their families.

Request a Care Package
If your spouse or loved one is deploying and you are a part of River of Life Church, you can request that monthly care packages are sent to your service member. These packages include practical items along with items for their spiritual growth.
