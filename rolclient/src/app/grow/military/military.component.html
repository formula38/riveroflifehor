MILITARY

Our divine calling is to stand in solidarity with our brave service members and their families, ensuring they remain tethered to the divine embrace of God and the fellowship of River of Life Church even amidst the trials of deployment.

At River of Life, our Military Deployed Support Group stands as a beacon of unity, tirelessly working to bridge the physical distance between our deployed heroes and their families through the distribution of care packages and regular communication. With each care package, prayerful consideration is given to include both practical essentials and spiritual nourishment, fostering strength and resilience in our service members.

Meet Our Leader
Led by the dedicated Shirley Russell, herself a devoted Marine Corps veteran's wife, our group embodies a fervent devotion to serving those who serve our nation. With a heart ablaze with love and empathy, Shirley leads our efforts in extending the loving arms of our church family to every service member and their cherished kin.

Request a Care Package
If you or your loved one is preparing for deployment and calls River of Life Church your spiritual home, we warmly invite you to avail yourselves of our care packages. Let us stand with you in prayer and provision, ensuring that no member of our military family feels isolated or forgotten during their time of service.
