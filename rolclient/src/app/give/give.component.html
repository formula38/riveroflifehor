GIVING

For where your treasure is, there your heart will be also.
- Matthew 6:21

Why We Give
God is generous and so he calls us to be as well.  What we do with what God has given us shows the world where our hearts are at and helps proclaim the gospel. We want to glorify God with every area of our lives, and that includes what we do with our finances.

Giving Changes Lives
We believe that God is into changing lives. He uses His people in the world to accomplish the work of preaching the gospel, feeding the hungry, and caring for those in need.

As we are blessed, we bless.

Giving is an outward expression of gratitude and love that we have for God. We want to worship God with all that we are and all that we have –and this includes our finances. Throughout Scripture we are instructed to give to God from the finances that He has entrusted to us and blessed us with.

Ways To Give

Give Online
You can give securely online using the form on this page, or through our mobile app.

Give In Person
You can also give in person at our church office or during one of our worship services using one of our envelopes. Simply ask an usher and they will be happy to provide one for you.

Mail A Check
If you'd like, you can mail a check in to the church office. You can use one of our pre-addressed offering envelopes to simplify the process.
