Mission Vision

Who We Are + What We Believe

The Great Commission
River of Life Church embraces the divine mandate bestowed upon us by Jesus Himself: "Go therefore and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit."

The Prophetic Vision
River of Life Church envisions a dynamic community of fervent believers, contributing to the transformation of our city through the profound impact of the gospel. We long to witness a city awakened as individuals encounter Jesus, embracing Him as their Lord and Savior.

Our Fundamental Convictions

Discover the bedrock of our faith.
River of Life Church holds unwavering convictions grounded in the timeless truths revealed in the Bible. All our teachings and ministries spring forth from these sacred doctrines.

The Divine Trinity
There exists one eternal God, manifest in three distinct persons: God the Father, God the Son, and God the Holy Spirit. He stands as the architect of all that is seen and unseen, deserving of boundless glory and praise. God, in His perfection, embodies love, power, holiness, goodness, knowledge, wisdom, justice, and mercy. Immutable, He remains the same yesterday, today, and forever.

Divine Revelation
God has unveiled Himself through His son, Jesus Christ, the visible manifestation of the unseen God, the holy scriptures, and the entirety of creation.

Humanity's Sacred Design
Both man and woman were fashioned in the divine image for God's glorification. The first humans, Adam and Eve, were created untarnished and entrusted with the stewardship of God's magnificent creations.

The Human Deviation
Yet, when Adam and Eve veered from obedience to God, they deviated from their intended nature, distorting the divine image within. This separation led to a rupture in communion with God, casting a shadow over all of creation from that moment onward.

Redemptive Salvation
Jesus Christ emerged to restore our connection with God. Living a sinless life, He willingly bore the weight of our transgressions on the cross. God resurrected Him, extending, by grace, the priceless gift of eternal life to all who, through faith, make Christ their Lord and Savior. Salvation, therefore, is exclusively found in Christ.

The Ekklesia
The Church stands as the visible embodiment of Christ, commissioned to glorify God and proclaim the gospel of Jesus Christ to the world.

The Imminent Resurrection
Anticipating the return of Jesus Christ, we affirm His impending judgment upon the living and the departed, heralding the consummation of God's kingdom on earth.
