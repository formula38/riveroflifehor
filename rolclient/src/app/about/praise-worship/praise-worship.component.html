About 2460

Represent Jesus + your Church.
We are so excited to announce that we are opening River of Life ’s first bookstore, the 2460 Bookstore. We are thrilled to show you all the fresh merchandise, powerful books and learning materials, stuff for kids and adults alike …it is simply going to be awesome!

We decided on the name 2460 because of the address of our new church campus at 2460 Gum Branch Road in Jacksonville.  We believe that this store is going to be much more than a place to buy cool things.  It will be a chance to spread the Gospel in a very practical way, grow in your faith, and connect with others.

We will be open on Sundays from 11:15am - 12:30pm and accept credit/debit cards, cash and check.  We will not be selling items online at this time.

We can't wait to meet you at the 2460 Bookstore soon!
