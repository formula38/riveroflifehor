Our Story

The Prophetic Journey...
PLANTED AND FLOURISHING

In the divine tapestry of time, the seeds of River of Life Church were sown in July 1991, as Minister Clifton Edwards, Jr. received ordination as a Pastor. Sent forth by his pastor and father, he embarked on a sacred mission to shepherd in the Antelope Valley. In August 1991, the Antelope Valley Christian Outreach Church emerged, with the Ramada Inn in Lancaster as the humble sanctuary for its initial services.

Under the spiritual guidance of Pastor Clifton Edwards, Jr., alongside Lady Tracie Edwards and their family, the Church blossomed through fervent evangelism and soul-winning endeavors. With each move orchestrated by God, the pastor diligently worked towards building His kingdom, and his faithfulness led to divine elevation.

On October 2, 2000, Bishop Edwards ascended to the esteemed position of District Overseer of the Antelope Valley Covenant District within the Full Gospel Baptist Church Fellowship. His unwavering dedication continued, and by July 2005, Overseer Clifton Edwards, Jr. assumed the responsibility of leading pastors. Simultaneously, he transformed the ministry's name to City of Zion, International, and on September 9 of the same year, he was elevated to the Episcopacy.

After two decades of pastoral service in the Antelope Valley, in 2011, Bishop and Lady Edwards relocated to Jacksonville, Florida. Their liturgical calling propelled them to minister and impact lives wherever they found themselves. In 2014, back in California, Bishop Edwards, with a faithful few, established the City of Zion Ministries in Sacramento, California, after the dissolution of the Greater Glory Deliverance Center.

Bishop Edwards' journey took another turn when he was chosen to lead Christ Temple Community Church in Yuba City. Following four years of dedicated labor, he proposed relocating the church to Sacramento, aligning with the majority of the congregation and their evangelistic goals. Teaming up with Pastor Michael Peavy of the Purpose Church, River of Life House of Restoration was inaugurated on Easter Sunday, April 1, 2018.

The unfolding chapters of Bishop Edwards' ministerial odyssey testify to the unwavering faith, resilience, and divine guidance that mark the River of Life Church's profound legacy.
