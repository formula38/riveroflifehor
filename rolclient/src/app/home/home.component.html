ABOUT US

River of Life Church House of Restoration is located in Sacramento the State Capital of California. We are a new church plant designed with You in mind! We are a compassionate caring ministry that wants to see lives changed by the power of the Gospel of our Lord and savior Jesus the Christ. The worship experience is approximately less than two hours in length, so you’ll have the remainder of your day to enjoy!
